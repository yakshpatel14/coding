What is StackIt?

StackIt is a minimal, clean, and community-driven Q&A forum platform where users can ask questions, post answers, and share knowledge across various topics. It is designed to encourage collaborative learning and provide structured, searchable knowledge sharing.

Key Features

- Ask and answer questions in a simple, intuitive interface.
- Chat-like experience to encourage quick responses.
- Secure login and registration for content contribution.
- Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js with a NeDB backend.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower learners and enthusiasts by providing a welcoming space to ask questions, explore solutions, and grow together. We believe learning is most effective when done together, and StackIt exists to support that journey.
